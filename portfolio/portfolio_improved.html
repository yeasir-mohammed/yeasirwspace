Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science graduate currently working as a Junior Data Analyst at BrainMachine.AI, where I handle end-to-end data workflows — from raw extraction and cleaning in SQL and Python, to building Power BI dashboards that help stakeholders make faster, better decisions.

My background in CSE gave me a solid grounding in algorithms, data structures, and database systems. Over time I've gravitated toward the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and applied machine learning — specifically building models that solve practical, domain-specific problems.
